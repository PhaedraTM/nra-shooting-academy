About the Instructor

Dedicated to helping citizens exercise their Second Amendment rights through safe, responsible education and hands-on firearm training.

Meet John Myseros

John Myseros is a Certified NRA Firearms Instructor with over 48 years of firearms experience and more than 20 years of teaching experience. Based in Fairfax, Virginia, John has trained hundreds of students in firearm safety, personal protection, and responsible ownership.

His classes are known for being clear, patient, and professional — designed to help every student, from beginners to experienced shooters, build confidence and skill while maintaining the highest standards of safety and respect for firearms.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with the knowledge, attitude, and skills necessary to safely and confidently own and operate firearms. We believe in the right to keep and bear arms as protected by the Constitution — and the responsibility that comes with that right.

“A well regulated militia, being necessary to the security of a free state, the right of the people to keep and bear arms, shall not be infringed.”
