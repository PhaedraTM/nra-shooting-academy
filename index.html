About the Instructor

John Myseros has spent a lifetime teaching and practicing safe firearms handling. He brings 44 years of firearms experience and 17 years of professional teaching to each class. John focuses on safety, confidence, and clear skill-building for students of all levels.

We provide handgun instruction (Basic Pistol, Home Firearm Safety, Personal Protection in the Home, First Steps Pistol) in Northern Virginia and the Washington, D.C., area. Our classes are small and hands-on — each live-fire class is run with a low student-to-instructor ratio to give you one-on-one support on the range.

Mission: To help people exercise their 2nd Amendment rights responsibly through quality training that emphasizes safe habits, clear decision-making, and practical skills.
