Courses & Pricing

Hands-on, instructor-led training designed to build confidence, knowledge, and safety.

Available Courses

Basic Pistol Shooting

Learn the fundamentals of safe firearm operation, revolver and semi-automatic handling, and marksmanship. This 2-day course includes classroom training and live range instruction.

  • Fee: $185.00/student
  • Includes: Classroom + range time, ammunition (if using our firearm), materials, certificate
  • Location: Fairfax, VA — NRA HQ Range

Home Firearm Safety

A non-shooting course covering safe firearm storage, cleaning, and responsible handling. Ideal for families and first-time owners.

  • Fee: $75.00/student
  • Includes: Firearms for demo, textbook, certificate, eligibility for concealed carry
  • Duration: 4 hours

Personal Protection in the Home

Learn how to protect yourself and your family using safe, defensive firearm practices. Includes both classroom and range time.

  • Fee: $200.00/student
  • Includes: Instruction, range time, textbook, 1 hour with police officer
  • Duration: 8 hours

First Steps Pistol

A fast-track introduction to safe pistol operation and ownership. Great for those seeking foundational skills and concealed carry eligibility.

  • Duration: 3–4 hours
  • Includes: Range time, ammo (if using our firearm), materials, targets

Ready to Take a Course?

All NRA-certified classes meet Virginia requirements for concealed carry permits.
